template<typename I>
struct boost::hana::Enumerable::instance< I, when< is_an< IntegralConstant, I >()> >

Instance of Enumerable for IntegralConstants.

This instance of Enumerable reflects the usual definition of succ and pred for natural numbers. Specifically, succ(n) is an IntegralConstant holding ++value(n), and pred(n) is an IntegralConstant holding --value(n).

Example

BOOST_HANA_CONSTANT_ASSERT(
succ(integral_constant<Integral, int, 2>)
==
integral_constant<Integral, int, 3>
);
BOOST_HANA_CONSTANT_ASSERT(
pred(integral_constant<Integral, int, 2>)
==
integral_constant<Integral, int, 1>
);
