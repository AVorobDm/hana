template<typename T, detail::std::size_t N>
struct boost::hana::ext::BuiltinArray< T, N >

Adapter for builtin arrays.

Provided models

  1. Searchable
    Builtin arrays whose size is known can be searched as-if they were homogeneous tuples. However, since arrays can only hold objects of a single type and the predicate to find must return a compile-time Logical, the find method is fairly useless. For similar reasons, the lookup method is also fairly useless.
  2. Foldable
    Builtin arrays whose size is known can be folded as-if they were homogeneous tuples. However, note that builtin arrays can't be made more than Foldable (e.g. Iterable) because they can't be empty and they also can't be returned from functions.
