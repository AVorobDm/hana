template<typename T, detail::std::size_t N>
struct boost::hana::Foldable::instance< T[N]>

Instance of Foldable for array types.

Builtin arrays whose size is known can be folded as-if they were homogeneous tuples.

Note
Builtin arrays can't be made more than Foldable (e.g. Iterable) because they can't be empty and they also can't be returned from functions.
